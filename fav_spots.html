SKARDU

Skardu is a city in the Gilgit-Baltistan region of northern Pakistan. It is the capital of the Skardu District and is located at an altitude of 2,438 meters (7,999 feet) above sea level. Skardu is surrounded by some of the world's highest mountains, including K2, the second-highest mountain in the world. The city is known for its stunning natural beauty, with rugged mountains, crystal-clear lakes, and lush green valleys. It is a popular tourist destination, particularly for mountaineers and trekkers who come to explore the nearby mountains and trekking routes. The area is also home to several ancient Buddhist rock carvings and petroglyphs, dating back to the 8th century.

HUNZA

Hunza is a mountainous valley in the Gilgit-Baltistan region of northern Pakistan, located at an altitude of around 2,500 meters (8,200 feet) above sea level. It is known for its stunning natural beauty, with towering snow-capped mountains, glaciers, and clear blue rivers. The people of Hunza are known for their longevity and good health, and the valley has been the subject of numerous studies on the health benefits of their diet and lifestyle. The local people primarily rely on agriculture, and the valley is known for its production of fruits, particularly apricots.

SWITZERLAND

Switzerland is a landlocked country in central Europe, bordered by Germany to the north, Austria and Liechtenstein to the east, Italy to the south, and France to the west. It is known for its stunning natural beauty, including the Swiss Alps, picturesque lakes, and quaint villages. Switzerland is a federal republic with a system of direct democracy. The country has a highly developed economy and is known for its banking, finance, and tourism industries. Switzerland is also home to numerous international organizations, including the United Nations, the International Committee of the Red Cross, and the World Trade Organization.

TURKY ISTANBUL

Istanbul is a vibrant city located in Turkey, situated at the intersection of Europe and Asia. It is the largest city in Turkey and the country's economic, cultural, and historical center. The city spans across two continents and is divided by the Bosphorus Strait, which connects the Black Sea and the Sea of Marmara. Istanbul has a rich history and was once the capital of the Byzantine and Ottoman empires. The city is home to numerous historical sites, including the Hagia Sophia, the Blue Mosque, and the Topkapi Palace, which are popular tourist destinations. The city also has a rich cultural heritage, with museums, art galleries, and theaters showcasing the country's art, music, and performing arts.

CALIFORNIA

California is a state located in the western region of the United States. It is the most populous state in the country and the third-largest state by area. The state is known for its diverse geography, including coastal beaches, redwood forests, mountain ranges, and deserts. California is home to some of the world's most renowned cities, such as Los Angeles, San Francisco, and San Diego. Los Angeles is the entertainment capital of the world, known for its film and television industry, while San Francisco is known for its iconic Golden Gate Bridge, cable cars, and diverse cultural scene.

MAKKAH

Makkah, also spelled Mecca, is a city located in the western region of Saudi Arabia. It is the holiest city in Islam and the birthplace of the Prophet Muhammad. It is home to the Masjid al-Haram, the largest mosque in the world, which surrounds the Kaaba, the most sacred site in Islam. Makkah is the site of the annual Hajj pilgrimage, which is considered one of the Five Pillars of Islam and is required of all able-bodied Muslims who can afford it. The Hajj takes place during the Islamic month of Dhu al-Hijjah and involves a series of rituals, including the Tawaf, or circumambulation of the Kaaba, and the Stoning of the Devil.
