LAHORE

Lahore is a city located in the province of Punjab, Pakistan. It is the second-largest city in Pakistan and is considered the cultural, historical, and educational hub of the country. Lahore has a rich history dating back to the Indus Valley Civilization and has been ruled by various empires including the Mughals, Sikhs, and British.

KARACHI

Karachi is the largest city in Pakistan and is located on the southern coast of the country, bordering the Arabian Sea. It is also the capital of the province of Sindh. With a population of over 14 million people, it is one of the most populous cities in the world. Karachi has a rich history dating back to ancient times, and it has been ruled by various empires and dynasties, including the Arabs, Mughals, and British. The city has a diverse population, with people from various ethnic and religious backgrounds living together.

ISLAMABAD

Islamabad is the capital city of Pakistan, located in the northern part of the country in the Islamabad Capital Territory. The city was designed in the 1960s by a Greek architect, and it is known for its modern and well-planned layout, with wide streets, green spaces, and modern architecture. Islamabad is home to many important government buildings and institutions, including the Parliament House, the Supreme Court, and the Presidential Palace. It is also the headquarters of many international organizations, including the United Nations Development Programme (UNDP) and the World Health Organization (WHO)..

KASHMIR

Kashmir is a region located in the northern part of the Indian subcontinent. It is divided between three countries: India, Pakistan, and China. The region is known for its natural beauty, with snow-capped mountains, scenic valleys, and crystal-clear lakes. The Indian-administered part of Kashmir is known as Jammu and Kashmir, and it is a popular tourist destination, with many scenic locations such as Srinagar, Pahalgam, and Gulmarg. The region is known for its beautiful lakes, such as Dal Lake, and for its gardens, including the Mughal Gardens of Shalimar Bagh and Nishat Bagh..
