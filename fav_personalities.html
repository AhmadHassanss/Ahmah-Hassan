ELON MUSK

Elon Musk is a billionaire entrepreneur and business magnate who has founded several successful companies, including Tesla, SpaceX, Neuralink, and The Boring Company. He was born on June 28, 1971, in Pretoria, South Africa. Musk moved to the United States in the 1990s and attended the University of Pennsylvania, where he earned degrees in physics and economics. He then co-founded Zip2, a company that provided business directories and maps to newspapers, which was eventually sold for nearly $300 million. Musk went on to co-found PayPal, an online payment system, which was later acquired by eBay for $1.5 billion. He then turned his attention to space exploration and founded SpaceX in 2002, with the goal of making space travel more affordable and accessible.

Abdul SATTAR Edhi

Abdul Sattar Edhi was a renowned Pakistani philanthropist and social activist, who dedicated his life to helping the poor and the needy. He was born on January 1, 1928, in Gujarat, India and passed away on July 8, 2016, in Karachi, Pakistan. Edhi founded the Edhi Foundation, which is one of the largest non-profit organizations in Pakistan, providing a range of social services, including free medical care, orphanages, and emergency services. He also established the world's largest volunteer ambulance network in Pakistan, which has saved countless lives.

IMRAN KHAN

Imran Khan is a Pakistani politician, former cricketer, and current Prime Minister of Pakistan. He was born on October 5, 1952, in Lahore, Pakistan. Khan began his cricketing career in the 1970s and quickly became one of Pakistan's most successful cricket players. He led the national team to victory in the 1992 Cricket World Cup, which remains one of Pakistan's greatest sporting achievements. After retiring from cricket, Khan became involved in politics and founded the Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI) political party in 1996. He ran for the office of Prime Minister multiple times but was unsuccessful until the 2018 general election, when his party won a majority of seats in the National Assembly, and he was sworn in as Prime Minister in August 2018.

QUAID-e-AZAM

Quaid-e-Azam Muhammad Ali Jinnah was the founder and first Governor-General of Pakistan. He was born on December 25, 1876, in Karachi, which was then part of British India and passed away on September 11, 1948, in Karachi, Pakistan. Jinnah was a prominent lawyer and politician who played a leading role in the movement for the independence of India from British colonial rule. He became a key figure in the All India Muslim League and advocated for the creation of a separate Muslim state in India, which ultimately led to the creation of Pakistan in 1947.
